Financial Aid Clerk

Cosumnes River College, part of the Los Rios Community College District, is dedicated towards providing resources and services to support students and the community as a whole in having their goals through related quality services and programs. As a Financial Aid Clerk, working for the Financial Aid Office of the Cosumnes River College, I played a vital role in guiding students through the financial aid process. My responsibilities involved assisting students with their inquiries about their financial aid file, helping students get a better understanding of their Financial Aid, and advising students of financial aid policy and upcoming deadlines. With the usage of programs such as OnBase and PeopleSoft, I processed student financial aid files, managed financial aid documents, and analyzed and resolved issues within student's financial aid records by providing solutions to address the issues and when necessary, modifying a student's financial aid file to ensure students receive their financial aid in compliance with financial aid policies.
